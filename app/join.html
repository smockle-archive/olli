Join OLLI at Auburn as a general or academic member.

General Membership ($25)

Offered July through June
  • Use all Auburn University libraries
  • Audit select Auburn University courses
  • Ride Tiger Transit buses
  • Attend seminars and workshops
  • Participate in extracurricular activities and socials
  • Receive 10% discount at the Auburn University Bookstore

Academic Membership ($140–225)

Offered September through May

Includes all the benefits of general membership, and allows you to take an unlimited number of OLLI courses. OLLI courses are offered during three terms—fall, winter and spring. Each term is 21 weeks.

Membership fees vary based on length of membership:
  • A single-term academic membership is $140
  • A two-term academic membership is $165
  • A three-term academic membership is $225

Once payment is received, you will receive an email with a link to courses available for registration. If you purchase a multi-term academic membership, you will receive a new email before each term.

Financial Aid

Our mission is to cultivate a community of lifelong learners. If financial issues prevent you from becoming a member of OLLI at Auburn, please fill out our Scholarship Application form. Financial assitance may be available.
